Our Services

Trenchless, No‑Dig Pipe Repair Services For Homes & Businesses in Celina, TX

We specialize in modern, trenchless solutions that repair or replace damaged sewer and water lines from the inside—saving your yard, driveway, and time while keeping costs under control.

Professional Trenchless Services

Every service is designed to minimize digging, shorten repair times, and reduce disruption to your property while delivering long‑lasting results.

No-Dig

Trenchless Sewer Line Repair

We repair cracked, offset, or root‑damaged sewer lines using trenchless methods that access the pipe from small entry points. Your yard, landscaping, and hardscapes stay virtually untouched while flow is fully restored.

Fast

Water Line Leak Repair & Replacement

Hidden leaks and failing water lines are corrected using no‑dig techniques that reduce excavation to the bare minimum. Most residential water line projects are completed in 1–2 days with little disruption to your routine.

Cost-Saving

Cured‑In‑Place Pipe Lining (CIPP)

We install a seamless epoxy liner inside your existing pipe, creating a new, durable inner pipe without full replacement. This method is ideal for aging lines and significantly reduces overall project cost and downtime.

Diagnostic

Sewer Camera Inspection

High‑definition camera inspections allow us to pinpoint breaks, bellies, and root intrusion before we recommend any repair. You see exactly what is happening inside your lines, helping you avoid unnecessary digging and guesswork.

Preventive

Drain Cleaning & Root Removal

We clear blockages, grease buildup, and invasive roots using professional equipment sized for residential and light commercial lines. Regular maintenance helps prevent backups and extends the life of your trenchless repairs.

Property-Safe

Residential & Small Commercial Solutions

From single‑family homes to small commercial buildings, we tailor each trenchless solution to pipe size, access, and soil conditions. Our crews work quickly and cleanly so you can get back to normal with minimal disruption.
